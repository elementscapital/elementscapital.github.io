About us

We are living in a world which is changing dramatically with innovative technology, and finance and investment industry are inevitably undergoing a transformation with the rising of artificial intelligence as well.

Our founders realized the trend is unstoppable and therefore we decide to create Elements to walk in the forefront of New Era rather than disappear in the memory of Old Times. By combining our expertise in value investing, macro asset allocation, quantitative finance and machine learning techniques, we are better equipped to make a new adventure altogether.
